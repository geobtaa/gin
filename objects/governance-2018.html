Structure

Steering Committee

  • Members: Project Lead, Project Metadata Coordinator, UMN Spatial Data Analyst & Curator, UMN Application Developer, Committee Chairs, [Task Force members on SLG], Product Owner

Committees

  • Metadata, Interface, Collection Development, Communications
  • Formed to address ongoing issues and continue as long as needed
  • Make recommendations to the Steering Committee
  • Interface Committee will help inform decisions made by Product Owner (and also in charge of UX)

Working Groups

  • Formed to address specific issues and then they sunset
  • Can be charged by Steering Committee or other Committee

Product Owner

The Product Owner will be the most active participant responsible for guiding the development of the BTAA geoportal. The primary responsibilities for this role encompass acting as:

  • a liaison between IT, Task Force Members, the Project Lead, and geoportal users
  • a decision maker about technical workflows and website implementations

Project Lead

The Project Lead is ultimately responsible for the success of the project and is responsible to the Project Sponsors and stakeholders. The primary responsibilities also include:

  • Chairing the Steering Committee
  • Acting as a liaison between the Project Sponsors and the Task Force
  • Setting and monitoring budgets        
  • Compiling monthly reports for the Project Sponsors
