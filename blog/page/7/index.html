    Featured Item: Border-to-Border Trail, Washtenaw County, Michigan.

    By Caroline Kayko

    What is the item?

    This item is a geospatial dataset depicting the Border-To-Border (B2B) Trail in Washtenaw County, MI in both shapefile and geodatabase file form. The B2B Trail is a non-motorized trail that stretches across Washtenaw County and connects cities, parks, and other destinations. It is currently still under construction, which is reflected in the dataset.

    By Kathleen Weessies

Trails are a perfect example: The state government and a local government will be paying attention to the trails under their own care.     Booklovers' map of the British Isles

    By Laura McElfresh

    Featured Item: Booklovers' map of the British Isles

    What is the item?

    This map, created by Paul M. Paine in 1927, shows literary landmarks throughout the British Isles, with magnified inset maps of London and Edinburgh. It includes places where significant events -- both real and fictional -- happened, plus birthplaces and homes of prominent authors. Some places on the map are shown as little pictures, like Sherwood Forest and the Globe Theatre.
